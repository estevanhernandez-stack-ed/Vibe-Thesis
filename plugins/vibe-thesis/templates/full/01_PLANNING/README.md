# 01_PLANNING

Pre-writing artifacts. The work that decides what the thesis is *about* before you start drafting prose.

## Suggested files

| File | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| `proposal.md` | The thesis proposal — what you're arguing, why it matters, what's in scope, what's not. |
| `outline.md` | The structural outline — chapters or sections in planned order, with one-paragraph intent per. |
| `claims-map.md` | Every load-bearing claim → planned evidence. Lets you spot orphan claims and overweighted citations early. |

These are starting suggestions; rename, add, or remove to fit your project.

## When to update

Re-visit `outline.md` and `claims-map.md` after every research swarm (see `04_AGENT_SWARMS/`). New evidence reshapes what the thesis can defend.
